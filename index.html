About Me:

I enlisted in the military, at a young age, right out of high school. I learned a lot throughout my years serving this nation, especially communication. As the years progressed I learned this invaluable trait through cohesion in a team, leading a team, and supervising multiple teams. This trait was also invaluable overseas in countries with different languages and cultural traits. Interacting, relating, and providing solutions can be difficult in our modern work environments. I pride myself in understanding this and working through solutions with different groups of people. This in itself is the crux, there is no one way to fix every problem, nor is there only one way to solve a specific problem, it is what works best for the people involved.
