I am a full stack software developer based out of South Dakota. My specialty is full-stack software development. My process starts with understanding the client and the problem they are facing. About Me:

I enlisted in the military, at a young age, right out of high school. I learned a lot throughout my years serving this nation, especially communication. As the years progressed I learned this invaluable trait through cohesion in a team, leading a team, and supervising multiple teams. This trait was also invaluable overseas in countries with different languages and cultural traits. Interacting, relating, and providing solutions can be difficult in our modern work environments. I pride myself in understanding this and working through solutions with different groups of people. This in itself is the crux, there is no one way to fix every problem, nor is there only one way to solve a specific problem, it is what works best for the people involved. My last position in the military was as an instructor, I have a passion for this, I was able to put these best practices to work and demonstrate to a versatile audience the importance of this skill. After retirement from the military I worked several different fields, continued my higher education, and I explored my passions. I have gained a sincere fondness for programming. I believe through proper implementation programming can change people, it can change work environments, and it can change the world.
